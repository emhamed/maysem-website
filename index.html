Maysem is a highly qualified MSc Aesthetic Medicine Pharmacist with many years of experience, combining strong clinical expertise. She is a holder of a Level 7 Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine with extensive experience in anti-wrinkle treatments, dermal fillers, and advanced injectable procedures.

Trained with leading institutions including the British Aesthetic Academy, Derma Institute, FOX Pharma Academy, and Fillmed UK Laboratories, Maysem specialises in advanced skin regenerative injectables such as Biostimulators. She is skilled in a wide range of aesthetic treatments including Sculptra, PRP, Polynucleotides, skin boosters, fat-dissolving treatments, microneedling, vitamin injections, and chemical peels.

With a strong focus on patient consultation, safety, and high-quality outcomes, Maysem is dedicated to providing exceptional aesthetic care.
